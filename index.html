In Lego you can build anything you want to build. For example cars, houses, helicopter and other things. If you see something and want to make model of it you can build it with Lego. Lego (/ˈlɛɡoʊ/ LEG-oh, Danish: [ˈle̝ːgo];[1][2] stylised as LEGO) is a line of plastic construction toys that are manufactured by The Lego Group, a privately held company based in Billund, Denmark. The company's flagship product, Lego, consists of variously coloured interlocking plastic bricks accompanying an array of gears, figurines called minifigures, and various other parts.
